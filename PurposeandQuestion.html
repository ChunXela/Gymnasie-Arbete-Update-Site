QUESTION

Does the strength of the print from a Prusa i3 mk3s differ compared to a similarly priced self-built large format printer when put to a stress test?

PURPOSE

The purpose of this research is to find out if the qualities of the prints from each printer differ and thus finding out if it more efficient to build your own 3d printer then buying a pre-built 3d printer from marketable brands like Prusa.
